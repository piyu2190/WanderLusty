About France

France is renowned for its rich history, art, and culture. From the romantic streets of Paris to the stunning landscapes of Provence, every region offers a unique experience that captures the heart of travelers.

Activities and Famous Places

Relax in the Gardens of Versailles

Explore the stunning gardens of the Palace of Versailles, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Explore the Louvre Museum

Discover world-famous art pieces, including the Mona Lisa at the Louvre Museum.

Stroll through Montmartre

Wander through the charming streets of Montmartre and enjoy its artistic vibe.

Visit the Eiffel Tower

Experience the breathtaking views from the top of the iconic Eiffel Tower.

Trip Cost Breakdown for 7 Days from India

  • Flight Costs: The cost for a round-trip flight from India to France varies, often ranging from approximately ₹40,000 to ₹60,000, depending on factors such as the airline, the season, and the booking time.
  • Accommodation:
    • Hostels: Hostels provide affordable options for budget travelers, generally costing around 30-60 EUR per night (roughly 2,700 to 5,400 INR).
    • Airbnb: Budget-friendly options start at 50-120 EUR per night (4,500 to 10,800 INR) for a room or small apartment.
    • Hotels: Mid-range hotels in popular cities like Paris or Nice generally cost between 100-250 EUR per night (9,000 to 22,500 INR).
  • Food: Daily food budget: Most travelers spend about €40 per day on food. This covers a mix of meals, often with breakfast being the least expensive and dinner the most.
    • Typical Meal Costs:
      • Breakfast: Roughly €8, with options like croissants and coffee.
      • Lunch: Around €15 for casual or quick meals.
      • Dinner: Approximately €25 if dining in a modest restaurant.
  • Activities:
    • Eiffel Tower Ticket: A ticket to the summit costs about €25, which is approximately ₹2,250.
    • Louvre Museum: Entrance fees are around €17, or ₹1,530.
    • Versailles Gardens: Entry to the gardens is free, but guided tours may cost around €20-30, which is ₹1,800 to ₹2,700.
  • Transportation: For a 7-day transportation plan in France, a Paris Visite pass costs about €65 (around ₹5,800) for unlimited travel on public transport within Paris.
  • Total Estimated Cost:
    • Budget Trip (Hostel, Basic Activities): ₹1,20,000 - ₹1,70,000
    • Mid-range Trip (Airbnb/Hotel, Multiple Activities): ₹2,00,000 - ₹2,80,000
