About England

England is a country rich in history and culture, known for its iconic landmarks, charming villages, and vibrant cities. From the bustling streets of London to the serene countryside, England offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity.

Trip Cost Breakdown for 7 Days from India

  • Flight Costs: Round-trip flights from India to England typically range from ₹45,000 to ₹75,000, depending on the airline and season.
  • Accommodation:
    • Hostels: Budget hostels can cost around £20-£50 per night (approximately ₹2,000 to ₹5,000).
    • Airbnb: Affordable options start at £40-£100 per night (around ₹4,000 to ₹10,000).
    • Hotels: Mid-range hotels usually cost between £80-£200 per night (roughly ₹8,000 to ₹20,000).
  • Food: Daily food budget is about £30-£50, covering meals from casual dining to pub food.
    • Typical Meal Costs:
      • Breakfast: Around £7 for a full English breakfast.
      • Lunch: Approximately £10-£15 for a casual meal.
      • Dinner: About £20-£30 in a restaurant.
  • Activities:
    • Tower of London Ticket: Entry costs around £30 (approximately ₹3,000).
    • Stonehenge Ticket: Admission is about £20 (around ₹2,000).
    • Lake District Activities: Various activities, including boat rentals, can cost around £15-£30.
  • Transportation: A rail pass for unlimited travel within England for 7 days costs about £150 (around ₹15,000).
  • Total Estimated Cost:
    • Budget Trip (Hostel, Basic Activities): ₹1,40,000 - ₹1,90,000
    • Mid-range Trip (Airbnb/Hotel, Multiple Activities): ₹2,10,000 - ₹3,00,000
