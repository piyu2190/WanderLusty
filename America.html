About America

Trip Cost Breakdown for 7 Days from India

  • Flight Costs: Round-trip flights from India to America typically range from ₹50,000 to ₹90,000, depending on the airline and season.
  • Accommodation:
    • Hostels: Budget hostels can cost around $30-$70 per night (approximately ₹2,500 to ₹5,500).
    • Airbnb: Affordable options start at $50-$150 per night (around ₹4,000 to ₹12,000).
    • Hotels: Mid-range hotels usually cost between $100-$250 per night (roughly ₹8,000 to ₹20,000).
  • Food: Daily food budget is about $40-$70, covering meals from casual dining to fine dining.
    • Typical Meal Costs:
      • Breakfast: Around $10 for a typical American breakfast.
      • Lunch: Approximately $15-$25 for a casual meal.
      • Dinner: About $25-$50 in a restaurant.
  • Activities:
    • Statue of Liberty Ticket: Entry costs around $25 (approximately ₹2,000).
    • Grand Canyon Tour: Guided tours can range from $50 to $150 (around ₹4,000 to ₹12,000).
    • Yellowstone National Park Entry: Admission is about $35 (around ₹3,000).
  • Transportation: A rail pass for unlimited travel within certain regions can cost about $150 (around ₹12,000).
  • Total Estimated Cost:
    • Budget Trip (Hostel, Basic Activities): ₹1,50,000 - ₹2,00,000
    • Mid-range Trip (Airbnb/Hotel, Multiple Activities): ₹2,50,000 - ₹3,50,000
