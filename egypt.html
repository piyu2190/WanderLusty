About Egypt

Egypt, known for its ancient civilization and some of the world's most famous monuments, is a country located in the northeastern corner of Africa. From the Pyramids of Giza to the temples of Luxor, Egypt offers a rich history and a unique cultural experience for every traveler.

Activities and Famous Places

Visit the Pyramids of Giza

Explore the iconic Pyramids of Giza, one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.

Explore Luxor Temple

Visit the magnificent Luxor Temple, a stunning example of ancient Egyptian architecture.

Visit the Valley of the Kings

Discover the tombs of ancient Egyptian pharaohs in the Valley of the Kings.

Explore the Nile River

Take a cruise on the Nile River and enjoy the stunning views of ancient temples and landscapes.

Trip Cost Breakdown for 7 Days from India

  • Flight Costs: The cost for a round-trip flight from India to Egypt typically ranges from approximately ₹30,000 to ₹60,000, depending on the airline, season, and booking time.
  • Accommodation:
    • Hostels: Budget hostels generally cost around 300-600 EGP per night (roughly 1,200 to 2,500 INR).
    • Airbnb: Affordable Airbnb options start at around 600-1,200 EGP per night (2,500 to 5,000 INR).
    • Hotels: Mid-range hotels in popular cities like Cairo or Luxor typically cost between 1,200-2,500 EGP per night (5,000 to 10,000 INR).
  • Food: Most travelers spend about 100-200 EGP per day on food, covering a mix of meals.
    • Breakfast: Roughly 20 EGP.
    • Lunch: Around 40 EGP.
    • Dinner: Approximately 80 EGP.
  • Activities:
    • Pyramids of Giza Tour: Approximately 200 EGP.
    • Nile River Cruise: Costs around 300-600 EGP.
    • Valley of the Kings Tour: Typically around 150-300 EGP.
    • Luxor Temple Entry: Costs about 100 EGP.
  • Transportation: Local transportation costs vary, but a 7-day public transport pass in Cairo is about 50 EGP.
  • Total Estimated Cost:
    • Budget Trip (Hostel, Basic Activities): ₹70,000 - ₹1,00,000
    • Mid-range Trip (Airbnb/Hotel, Multiple Activities): ₹1,20,000 - ₹1,80,000
