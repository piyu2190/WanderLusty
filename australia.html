Trip Cost Breakdown for 7 Days from India

  • Flight Costs: The cost for a round-trip flight from India to Australia typically ranges from approximately ₹40,000 to ₹70,000, depending on the airline, season, and booking time.
  • Accommodation:
    • Hostels: Budget hostels generally cost around 1,000-2,500 AUD per night (roughly 20,000 to 50,000 INR).
    • Airbnb: Affordable Airbnb options start at around 2,500-5,000 AUD per night (50,000 to 1,00,000 INR).
    • Hotels: Mid-range hotels in popular cities like Sydney or Melbourne typically cost between 5,000-10,000 AUD per night (1,00,000 to 2,00,000 INR).
  • Food: Most travelers spend about 1,500 AUD per day on food, covering a mix of meals.
    • Breakfast: Roughly 200 AUD.
    • Lunch: Around 300 AUD.
    • Dinner: Approximately 1,000 AUD.
  • Activities:
    • Sydney Opera House Tour: Approximately 40 AUD.
    • Great Barrier Reef Snorkeling: Costs around 200-400 AUD.
    • Outback Tours: Typically around 150-300 AUD.
    • Melbourne Laneways Tour: Costs about 50-100 AUD.
  • Transportation: Local transportation costs vary, but a 7-day public transport pass in Sydney is about 60 AUD.
  • Total Estimated Cost:
    • Budget Trip (Hostel, Basic Activities): ₹1,00,000 - ₹1,50,000
    • Mid-range Trip (Airbnb/Hotel, Multiple Activities): ₹1,80,000 - ₹2,50,000
