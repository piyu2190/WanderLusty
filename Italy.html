About Italy

Italy is famous for its art, history, and cuisine. From the ancient ruins of Rome to the romantic canals of Venice, each region offers a rich tapestry of experiences that delight travelers from around the world.

Trip Cost Breakdown for 7 Days from India

  • Flight Costs: The cost for a round-trip flight from India to Italy varies, often ranging from approximately ₹40,000 to ₹70,000, depending on factors such as the airline, the season, and the booking time.
  • Accommodation:
    • Hostels: Hostels provide affordable options for budget travelers, generally costing around 30-60 EUR per night (roughly 2,700 to 5,400 INR).
    • Airbnb: Budget-friendly options start at 50-120 EUR per night (4,500 to 10,800 INR) for a room or small apartment.
    • Hotels: Mid-range hotels in popular cities like Rome or Florence generally cost between 100-250 EUR per night (9,000 to 22,500 INR).
  • Food: Daily food budget: Most travelers spend about €40 per day on food. This covers a mix of meals, often with breakfast being the least expensive and dinner the most.
    • Typical Meal Costs:
      • Breakfast: Roughly €8, with options like pastries and coffee.
      • Lunch: Around €15 for casual or quick meals.
      • Dinner: Approximately €25 if dining in a modest restaurant.
  • Activities:
    • Colosseum Ticket: A ticket to the Colosseum costs about €18, which is approximately ₹1,620.
    • Gondola Ride in Venice: A gondola ride typically costs around €80 for 30 minutes, or ₹7,200.
    • Leaning Tower of Pisa: Entrance fees are around €18, or ₹1,620.
  • Transportation: For a 7-day transportation plan in Italy, a rail pass for unlimited travel between major cities costs about €150 (around ₹13,500).
  • Total Estimated Cost:
    • Budget Trip (Hostel, Basic Activities): ₹1,30,000 - ₹1,80,000
    • Mid-range Trip (Airbnb/Hotel, Multiple Activities): ₹2,00,000 - ₹2,80,000
