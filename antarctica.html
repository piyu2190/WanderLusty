Explore the Wonders of Antarctica!

Traveling to Antarctica from India is an adventure of a lifetime!

You can embark on your journey via:

  • Argentina:
    • Fly from India to Buenos Aires.
    • Take a domestic flight to Ushuaia.
    • Board an Antarctic cruise from Ushuaia, typically lasting 10 to 20 days.
  • Chile:
    • Fly from India to Santiago.
    • Take a domestic flight to Punta Arenas.
    • Fly to King George Island and board an expedition ship to Antarctica.
  • South Africa:
    • Fly from India to Cape Town.
    • Find a cruise departing for Antarctica from Cape Town.
    • Be prepared for a longer journey at sea.

Ensure you check visa requirements and book your trip in advance for an unforgettable experience!
