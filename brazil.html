About Brazil

Brazil is the largest country in South America, known for its vibrant culture, stunning landscapes, and diverse wildlife. From the Amazon rainforest to the beaches of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil offers a unique experience for every traveler.

Activities and Famous Places

Visit Christ the Redeemer

Marvel at the iconic Christ the Redeemer statue overlooking Rio de Janeiro.

Explore the Amazon Rainforest

Experience the biodiversity of the Amazon rainforest on a guided tour.

Relax on Copacabana Beach

Enjoy the sun and vibrant atmosphere at the famous Copacabana Beach in Rio de Janeiro.

Visit Iguazu Falls

Witness the breathtaking beauty of Iguazu Falls, one of the largest and most impressive waterfalls in the world.

Trip Cost Breakdown for 7 Days from India

  • Flight Costs: The cost for a round-trip flight from India to Brazil typically ranges from approximately ₹50,000 to ₹90,000, depending on the airline, season, and booking time.
  • Accommodation:
    • Hostels: Budget hostels generally cost around 1,000-2,500 BRL per night (roughly 20,000 to 50,000 INR).
    • Airbnb: Affordable Airbnb options start at around 2,500-5,000 BRL per night (50,000 to 1,00,000 INR).
    • Hotels: Mid-range hotels in popular cities like Rio de Janeiro or São Paulo typically cost between 5,000-10,000 BRL per night (1,00,000 to 2,00,000 INR).
  • Food: Most travelers spend about 150-300 BRL per day on food, covering a mix of meals.
    • Breakfast: Roughly 30 BRL.
    • Lunch: Around 50 BRL.
    • Dinner: Approximately 100 BRL.
  • Activities:
    • Christ the Redeemer Tour: Approximately 70 BRL.
    • Amazon Rainforest Tour: Costs around 200-400 BRL.
    • Iguazu Falls Tour: Typically around 150-300 BRL.
    • Copacabana Beach Activities: Costs about 50-100 BRL.
  • Transportation: Local transportation costs vary, but a 7-day public transport pass in Rio de Janeiro is about 50 BRL.
  • Total Estimated Cost:
    • Budget Trip (Hostel, Basic Activities): ₹1,20,000 - ₹1,80,000
    • Mid-range Trip (Airbnb/Hotel, Multiple Activities): ₹2,00,000 - ₹2,80,000
